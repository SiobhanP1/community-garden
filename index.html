Community Garden

Growing organic fruit and vegetables together in the heart of the city

What we do

We provide a place for the community to grow vegetables. Check out our social media for the latest details.


  • Occasional free classes and workshops
  • A place to grow and enjoy the (delicious) results
  • Gardeners of all ages and abilities welcome

Who we are

We are Jack and Marie, two lifelong gardeners with a passion for sharing our knowledge with others. We realise that not everyone has a garden and firmly believe that everyone has a right to fresh fruit and vegetables. We aim to provide a place for people to learn, to grow, to enjoy and appreciate nature, and good food too. We invite you to join us.
